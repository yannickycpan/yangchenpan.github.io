About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, Indiana University at Bloomington. My PhD advisor is Dr. Martha White. I am interested in reinforcement learning, sequential decision making, and their interesting applications. For sequential decision problems, I am particularly interested in the category of methods called "computational stochastic optimization." In some sense, reinforcement learning algorithms also fall in this category. Currently, I am mainly working on developing reinforcement learning algorithms.
